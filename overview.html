Who We Are

PRE-TAXES SOLUTIONS is a professionally managed tax consulting and business advisory firm registered under MSME (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises), with its head office located in Mumbai.

We are a dynamic team of Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, and GST Practitioners who combine technical excellence with a practical, solution-driven mindset. Our young and energetic professionals are committed to delivering expert services while maintaining the highest standards of professional ethics.

Our steady and structured approach to professional services has helped us build long-term, mutually beneficial relationships with our clients. We focus on accuracy, transparency, and timely execution to ensure complete peace of mind.
