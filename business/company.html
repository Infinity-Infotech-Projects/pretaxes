Company Formation Services

Start your business with the right legal structure and complete compliance. We provide end-to-end company formation services with expert guidance and transparent processes.

Private Limited Company Registration

A Private Limited Company is ideal for startups and growing businesses, offering limited liability, higher credibility, and ease of fundraising.

Key Requirements

  • Minimum 2 Directors & Shareholders
  • Director Identification Number (DIN)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • Registered Office Address

Registration Includes

  • Name Approval with MCA
  • MOA & AOA Drafting
  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • PAN & TAN Registration

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

LLP combines the flexibility of a partnership with the benefit of limited liability, suitable for professionals and service-based businesses.

Key Requirements

  • Minimum 2 Designated Partners
  • DPIN & DSC
  • LLP Agreement
  • Registered Office Address

Registration Includes

  • Name Reservation
  • LLP Incorporation Certificate
  • Agreement Filing
  • PAN Registration

One Person Company (OPC)

OPC is best suited for solo entrepreneurs who want full control with the benefits of a corporate structure.

Eligibility

  • Single Director & Shareholder
  • Indian Resident
  • Nominee Appointment

Benefits

  • Limited Liability
  • Separate Legal Entity
  • Easy Compliance
