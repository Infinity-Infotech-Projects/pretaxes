Why Motor Insurance Is Important

Motor insurance protects you from financial losses caused by accidents, theft, fire, or natural calamities.

It also provides mandatory third-party liability coverage as required by law, ensuring legal and financial safety.

Types of Motor Insurance

Car Insurance

Coverage for private and commercial cars against damage and theft.

Two-Wheeler Insurance

Protection for bikes and scooters from accidents and losses.

Third-Party Insurance

Mandatory coverage for third-party injury or property damage.

Comprehensive Insurance

Complete coverage for own damage plus third-party liabilities.
