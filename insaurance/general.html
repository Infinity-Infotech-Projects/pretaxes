Understanding General Insurance

General insurance protects you from financial losses arising due to accidents, theft, natural calamities, travel disruptions, or business liabilities.

Unlike life insurance, general insurance focuses on protecting your valuable assets and day-to-day risks, ensuring financial stability during unforeseen situations.

Types of General Insurance

Motor Insurance

Protection for cars and two-wheelers against accidents, theft, and damage.

Health Insurance

Coverage for medical expenses for individuals, families, and corporates.

Travel Insurance

Security against trip cancellations, delays, and medical emergencies.

Property Insurance

Safeguards homes, offices, and commercial buildings.

Business Insurance

Risk coverage for assets, employees, and liabilities.
